Hospitality & Condo-Hotel

Hotels, restaurants, and condo-hotel environments where atmosphere is the product, designed to feel effortless and engineered to perform.

Hospitality interiors live or die on experience. Every surface, sightline, and lighting decision shapes how a guest feels the moment they walk in. We design that arc deliberately, from arrival to the smallest in-room detail, while respecting the operational realities of a working property.

Our hospitality work spans restaurants and hotels, including our Miami project, Mimet. We treat each as a complete world with its own material story, then visualize it photoreal before a single finish is ordered.

What's Included

  • Guest-experience and atmosphere design
  • Restaurant, bar, and lobby concepts
  • Guestroom and suite design
  • FF&E direction and specification
  • Lighting and material storytelling
  • Photoreal 3D visualization for stakeholders
